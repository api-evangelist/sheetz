# Sheetz (sheetz)

Sheetz, Inc. is an American chain of convenience stores and coffee shops headquartered in Altoona, Pennsylvania. Operating more than 800 company-owned stores across Pennsylvania, West Virginia, Maryland, Ohio, Virginia, North Carolina, and Michigan, Sheetz is one of the largest convenience store chains in the United States. The company offers fuel, food service, coffee, and retail products, supported by a mobile app for ordering, rewards, and store locator services. Sheetz provides B2B EDI and API integration capabilities for suppliers and distribution partners.
